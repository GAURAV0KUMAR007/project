Game Rules


  Rock Paper Scissors is a simple game in which two players simultaneously choose one option from rock, paper, or scissors.The game is played in rounds, and the first player to win a predetermined number of rounds is declared the overall winner. Players can choose their options in any order, and the game is typically played in a best-of-three or best-of-five format.The game is often used as a simple decision-making tool or as a fun way to settle disputes. It is also a popular game in many cultures and is often played in informal settings, such as among friends or family members.
